Author: Ahmed Adel Al-Adawy

The novel: The mummy of Tel al-Fara'in

The Novel (Historical & Romance & Horror & Thriller & Suspense)

The events of the story revolve around the hero Hussein Abdel Tawab, who saw a weird machine with the junk collector's, bought this machine and found a missing piece in it to be operated as he knew it was a time machine, and when he found the missing piece in an abandoned palace, the machine took him to the early ages of Egyptian Pharaonic history Then he tried to return again and did not find the piece through which the device works again, he adapted to the conditions there and joined the king's army and when he found the piece he returned again with his girlfriend and married her and a black cat appeared in his life he wanted to get rid of him but she refused and the cat's behavior was strange towards him and when He wanted to get rid of him, his girlfriend disappeared, then he knew it was Jinn He wanted to forget what had happened, and when he fell in love with another woman, she started showing him his old girlfriend, and he wanted to get rid of her by going back in time
